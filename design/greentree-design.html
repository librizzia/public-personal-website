Greentree Design

For Greentree Church, I created a cohesive set of visual assets to enhance their communication and branding. This included announcement slides to inform the congregation of upcoming events, sermon slides to support weekly messages with clarity and professionalism, and lower third graphics for live streams and video content, adding a polished and engaging touch. Each design was tailored to reflect the church's welcoming and community-focused identity.
