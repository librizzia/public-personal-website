Coffee Corner Website Design

Coffee Corner is a cozy local coffee shop offering a warm and inviting atmosphere for its patrons. I designed their website to reflect their welcoming and community-focused identity, crafting brand colors, and designing each page and section with an attention to detail for these goals. The visual identity emphasizes simplicity and approachability, featuring clean but simple layouts and engaging graphics that highlight their menu, customer reviews, and location. The result is a polished and cohesive online presence that mirrors the charm and personality of Coffee Corner.
